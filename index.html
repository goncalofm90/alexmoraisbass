What is my Background?

I´m a proud student of different gigantic teachers !
Miguel Teixeira, Walter Lopes, Luís Moreno and Fernando Rui Amaral were my guitar teachers .

In Lisbon's National Music Conservatory, I studied Cello with the great cello master Andrej Michalczyk.
Then I studied with Luis Sá Pessoa in the school of the Lisbon metropolitan Orchestra having the privilege of pursuing my cello journey with private tuition with Miguel Rocha and Marina Freitas.

I Studied Jazz in Escola de Jazz do Barreiro for 3 years and then I had the opportunity to take private lessons with so many friends and excellent teachers like Massimo Cavalli, Yuri Daniel, Nuno Allan, Paulo Neves, Rui Teixeira (Double Bass), Guto Lucena, António Palma, Jorge Moniz, Miguel Barrosa, João Maurilío, Mário Delgado,Demian Cabauld just to name a few!

What services can I provide?

Guitar/bass private lessons? Harmony or theory?
I taught in several schools throughout my journey as a music teacher. From head of the music department and music teacher in Santo Antonio International School or guitar and theory teacher in Musicland I have experienced different challenges as a teacher and even as a form tutor dealing with students from all different ages!
And its gets funnier and better every new challenge as they come around!
Currently i teach several private students and teach a guitar course in St. James School where music is a valued subject and students get a lot from it!

Guitar stems? Bass Stems? Email me! I got different budgets to fit your project! From my private studio to my official partnership with Red Alert Studios I´m committed to find the guitar or bass line you need and suites your project exploring different aesthetics and styles from punk to reggae, from metal to pop, from jazz to hip-hop.....you name it!
If you want a fretless line, an analog synth bite from Electro Harmonix or a 5 stringer Low end, your creativity and need is the limit!

Call me any time!
